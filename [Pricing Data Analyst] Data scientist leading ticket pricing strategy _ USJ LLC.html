[ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES]

・Analysis of ticket prices ・Proposal of strategies
・Analysis of ticket promotion measures
・Proposal of optimal prices based on demand forecasts
・Process management related to the above

[REQUIREMENTS]

<Required>
・Ability to build predictive models and analyze data using Python and R 
・Ability to provide suggestions for decision-making based on predictions and analysis results 
・Ability to analyze and create documents using Excel and Tableau 
・Ability to create documents using PowerPoint 
・Business-level English skills to collaborate with staff at the US headquarters 

<Preferred>
・Experience in price analysis, revenue management, etc.
・Basic knowledge of consumer marketing
・Project management experience 

<Desired candidate>
・High level of ownership
・Strategic thinking
・Teamwork and collaboration
・Thought-based leadership
